Employment Accommodation Services

The Employment Accommodation Service, is a multi-disciplinary support team that is available to assist in the hiring, retraining, retention or advancement of persons with disabilities.

Services Available

  • Consultation Services
  • Work site Assessment
  • Equipment resources and co-ordination of equipment purchase and referrals
  • Reports documenting barriers to employment and recommendations for accommodation
  • Equipment Set-up and Staff training
  • Coordination with other services
  • Equipment trials and client compatibility testing
  • Information resources for adaptive technology
  • Website accessibility reviews and training
  • Equipment testing and evaluation

Team Members

The Employment Accommodation Service utilizes the expertise of individuals with a multi-disciplinary background. Team members include: Occupational Therapists, Vision Technology Specialists (authorizers of High Technology Vision Aids for the Assistive Devices Program, Ontario Ministry of Health), Computer programmers and Technicians with a specialty in developing and modifying products to enable individuals with disabilities to access technology.

Types of Technology

  • Closed Circuit Televisions (CCTVs)
  • Screen Magnification Software
  • Screen Reading Software
  • Alternative Keyboards and Mouse Systems
  • Voice Recognition software
  • Ergonomic workstation set-up
  • Refreshable Braille displays and Embossers

More information on people with disabilities in the workplace can be found on our Accommodating Employees with Disabilities page.
