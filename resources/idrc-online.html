IDRC Online

The IDRC site provides a wealth of information on the subject of Adaptive Technology. We have an extensive collection of research papers published by ATRC and IDRC staff available in full-text. We have also created a number of tutorials to aid in the installation and use of various common adaptive software packages.

A related site, SNOW (Special Needs Opportunity Window), is also an excellent resource for online information.
