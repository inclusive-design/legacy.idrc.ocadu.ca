The Accessible Information Kiosk Project

The ATRC designed an information kiosk that is accessible to users with disabilities. The accessible kiosk was implemented and tested in Robarts Library, at the University of Toronto.

The ATRC designed an information kiosk that is accessible to users with disabilities. The accessible kiosk was implemented and tested in Robarts Library, at the University of Toronto. The kiosk, located at the ground level, accessible entrance to the Libray provides text-based information about Accessibility and Disability Services including floor plans and a directory of departments within the Library.

This project is partially funded by the Information Commons, University of Toronto.
