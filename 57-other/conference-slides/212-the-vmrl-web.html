The VMRL Web

VRML at SGI

In this site you will find:

  • Download site for the VRML browser CosmoPlayer (now available for the Macintosh)


  • Information on the Cosmo Worlds and Cosmo Code software (now available for Win'95/NT)


  • A VRML gallery


  • Information on what SGI is doing with Java3d

Intervista

In this site you will find:

  • The download site for the Intervista VRML plugin


  • VRML basics and tutorials


  • Whitepapers on VRML


  • A VRML gallery

Other Nodes on the VRML Web

  • Always a good place to start is the FAQ.


  • Making VRML Accessible at the National Institute of Standards and Technology


  • Information about visualization and virtual environment technologies can be found at NIST


  • A great VRML resource and gallery can be found at the mining company website


  • A dynamic discusion of the VRML standard can be found at the www-vrml mailing list. (good luck getting through)


  • The VRML Repository at the SanDiego Supercomputer Center contains a very thorough list of links to VRML sites.


  • Stroud's CWSApps List has reviews and ranking for Windows 3.1 andWindows 95 VRML browsers.
